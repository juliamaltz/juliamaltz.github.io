Experience

I have had a series of jobs, internships, and volunteer opportunities that have helped to give me a well-rounded perspective of the marketing communications industry. I cannot wait to apply these skills to future opportunities after graduation. Below, I have highlighted a few positions that have given me excellent experience.

Bravo Group, Public Relations/Communications Intern

  • This position provided me with a well-rounded experience in all facets of a full-house public relations agency that supplies a multitude of services and represents a variety of sectors. Bravo Group adopts a campaign-style approach to help their clients win. They provide services in public relations, government relations, creative services, and audience research. As their intern, I participated directly with clients and garnered great communication skills. I helped to write press releases, media lists, social media and editorial content, as well as became involved with marketing research. I also had experience analyzing social media and traditional PR tactics

Breslow Partners, Public Relations Intern

  • In this internship I became introduced to the field of Public Relations and became instantly enamored with it, knowing this is what I wanted to pursue as a career. I interned for an in-house agency and worked closely with clients. I attended client meetings, assisted with creative campaign planning, gained excellent writing experience through press releases and media pitches.

Philadelphia Magazine, Special Events Marketing Intern

  • This internship served as my first experience in the marketing and event planning industries. I loved how fast-paced and dynamic everything was. Here I had the chance to participate in internal operations for several high-profile events of the magazine. I also got to attend the events themselves and assist with registration and other roles.
